Welcome KDR & Associates

 

KD Law Office is local law office duly registered since 2008 with Bar Association of Kingdom of Cambodia and changed to KDR & Associates Law Firm (KDR) in 2014.

Our goal is to be the firm of choice for our clients with respect to their most challenging legal issues, most significant business transactions and most critical investments.

Because we want to provide our clients with more than just outstanding legal services, we invest in getting to know the environments in which they operate. We offer our sector knowledge through our various cross-practice teams.

 

Our Services

 

KDR provides mains legal services in Cambodia such as :

  • Litigation in all court cases within Cambodia
  • Corporate and Taxation
  • Business, Investment and Trade Law Advisory
  • Intellectual Property Rights
  • Cadastral Registration and Real Estate Advisory
  • Khmer-foreign Marriage, Khmer nationality Application
  • Intellectual Property Rights Registration
  • Employment Agreement and Labor dispute Resolution
  • Legal Opinion and Services
